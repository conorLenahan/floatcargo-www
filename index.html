Build with Us

San Francisco Bay Area | Full Time | On-site

Float Cargo is building the next era of emissions-free, long-haul cargo logistics — starting with autonomous airships designed for high-efficiency freight.

We’re looking for founding engineers who thrive on first-principles thinking, cross-disciplinary design, and hands-on iteration. If you love building hardware that actually flies — this is your place.

What You’ll Do

  • Design, prototype, and test the first generation of flight hardware — from structural assemblies to power and control systems.
  • Move seamlessly between mechanical, electrical, and systems work: CAD, harness design, fabrication, bench testing, and field integration.
  • Own design decisions end-to-end: requirements, modeling, fabrication, and flight validation.
  • Help shape early engineering culture and methods — documentation, build processes, and our physical lab environment.
  • Work directly with the founder to translate ambitious performance targets into working vehicles — rapidly and safely.

What We’re Looking For

  • Deep comfort building physical systems from scratch — whether your background is ME with strong EE intuition, or EE with mechanical intuition.
  • Proficiency with CAD (Fusion 360 or SolidWorks), rapid prototyping, and basic machining or composites.
  • Familiarity with embedded electronics, motor controllers, or hybrid-electric powertrains is a plus.
  • Curiosity, rigor, and a bias toward action — you build, test, and learn quickly.
  • Excitement about working in a small, focused team where everything you touch flies within months.

What You’ll Find Here

  • A rare chance to define a new class of aircraft and reshape global logistics.
  • A workshop-based environment in San Francisco where we design, fabricate, and fly our prototypes ourselves.
  • Backing from 50 Years and a growing network of partners who believe in building for the long term.
